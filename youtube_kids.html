Youtube Kids Supercut

Premise

I feel like I was successful in my attempt to showcase the type of entertainment todays kids are exposed to on Youtube. Originally, my goal was to have more of a story/narrative piece, but that evolved into showcasing how Youtubers like Jake Paul interact with babies after finding out he collaborates with kid channels. These are 7 and 8 year old kids (sometimes even younger) being used as dolls to sell his merchandising. I don't know who is worse: Jake or the parents.
