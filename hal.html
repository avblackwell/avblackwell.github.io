HAL Terminal Website - It's Live Here!

Premise

I approached this project with story in mind. My first idea involved a recreation of an old windows operating system inside a browser, but I soon realized how much work that would take. I suffered a concussion from a nasty bike accident, and that severely cut into my time to work on this project; however, I still feel very confident in my work. Where it lacks in story, it makes up in style. My favorite aspect of the final website is the scrolling terminal. It drives home the point that HAL is alive.
