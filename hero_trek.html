Premise

You are the unnamed, backward-capped hero tasked with completing a new form of therapy. Since all you know is slaying dragons and saving princesses, the therapy is set up in the form of a dungeon tower with true peace (in the form of finding yourself) being at the top. All of your problems manifest into common dungeon monsters meaning you can use your cunning wits and exquisite jawline to slay each beast. Scattered throughout the tower are useful items including weapons, armor, and potions. Knowing the complexities of mental health issues and the therapeutic techniques used to deal with them, each run in the tower will be different. This can put off some adventurers, but not you. You embrace it head on!
