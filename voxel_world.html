Interactive Voxel World

Premise

For this project, I created my backyard growing up. The next best thing to going home would be to experience my backyard in unity. In my backyard, I will showcase my french bulldog puppy, a ball that he plays loves, and a big porch (with built in sitting rock). I will be creating this world with nostalgia goggles on high. My goal was to make the viewer experience my childhood.
